Sources & method

Hybrid parametric wind/current climatology (trade belts, ITCZ migration, monsoon reversal, westerlies + major currents) following the pilot-chart / CLIWOC 1750–1854 picture, driving square-rigger sailing polars through a least-time router, then calibrated to recorded at-sea durations.

Anchors: SlaveVoyages (Middle Passage), East-India company logs, Atlantic packet/merchant averages. Times are at-sea sailing figures (they exclude port calls, convoy waits and monsoon layovers).

Caveats: isochrones are climatological averages, not single voyages; westbound-against-the-westerlies and equatorial eastbound legs carry larger residuals; several routes are model-only.
